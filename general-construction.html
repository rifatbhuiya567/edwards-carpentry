General Construction & Building Works

At Edwards Carpentry & Construction we can happily manage any general construction or building project for you.

Right from groundworks and the laying of first foundations through services integration to interior decoration and finishing with second fix carpentry, flooring and appliances, we have the skills and capabilities to provide you with an end to end service.

Our General Building Works include:

  • - Extension: (to existing) Side returns, New floors & Roofs
  • - New Build: Traditional & timber frame builds
  • – Loft Conversion: Mansards & dormers
  • – Refurbishment: Complete commercial & residential refurbs
  • – Bathroom & Kitchen: Design, Supply & Fitting
  • – Carpentry: First & Second Fix Carpentry & Joinery
  • – Roofing and Flooring
  • – Plumbing, Electrical & tiling
