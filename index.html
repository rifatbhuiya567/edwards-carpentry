Welcome to Edwards Carpentry

Edwards Carpentry & Construction deliver a wide range of quality built solutions for our commercial and residential clients. We can undertake single story extensions to complete new builds or full house renovations and can supply all the trades you will ever need whilst project managing the whole job from start to finish.

Aside from our many happy customers & commercial clients, we also hold accreditations including certification for Asbestos Awareness and from the Construction Industry Training Board.
